Bio

Howdy!! My name is Spencer Callaway. I am a native Texan and Austinite. I live in the northern area of Austin close to Cedar Park and Leander. I am a Texas A&M graduate with a degree in plant and environmental soil science.

After college I couldn't find a job in my degree field without having to go back to college for an extended period of time. So I worked a few odd jobs starting with a emergency home restoration company, after that I worked for Life Storage as an assistant manager, and now I currently work part time at Twin Liquors while I'm completing a Coding BootCamp to become a full-stack web developer.

I am a musician and a gamer. I have several instruments and I have been a musician for close to 17 years. I game mostly on my PC but I also have a few console systems.
